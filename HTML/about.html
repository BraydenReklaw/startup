Mindmaps

Mindmaps are amongst the better methods of visually representing one's mindscape and thoughts, and are often represented in media through a convoluted mess of threads and paper on a bulletin board. Best used to explore relationships between ideas, mindmaps have been most recognizable in detective movies as a method of note tracking, such as in the movie Sherlock Holmes: A Game of Shadows.
